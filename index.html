  • Music Search using Query by Humming and Artificial Neural Networks

Abstract

People might not remember the lyrics of a random song that they would like to find, except for the tune of a certain part of the song. With the emerging technology of Music Information Retrieval, Query by Singing or Huming systems have been explored by researchers since the past decade.

With the use of Artificial Neural Networks classifier, rightful data collection, and feture extraction from music data set, the researchers of the study aim to develop a prototype that would allow real-time hummed queries as input on a music search system that would search within a databse.
